GEOOSE combines robotics, biological treatment, and renewable energy. Discover the core technologies that power our water quality monitoring system.

Unique Capabilities

Three key innovations that define the GEOOSE platform.

Coordinated Movement

Robotic units communicate to coordinate their paths. This ensures complete area coverage and efficient movement without needing a human operator.

Pollutant Treatment

Specialized capsules deploy to treat contaminants in the water. These eco-friendly units dissolve completely while targeting pollutants.

Autonomous docking stations with solar panels enable continuous 24/7 operation, automatic charging, and data uploading.
